About The Induction

Your first step towards an exciting journey in Cybersecurity & Digital Forensics.

Welcome to the official induction programme of the FY B.Sc Cybersecurity & Digital Forensics course. This session is specially organised to introduce you to the department, faculty members, curriculum, laboratories, career opportunities, student activities, and your seniors. Join us as we begin an exciting academic journey together.
